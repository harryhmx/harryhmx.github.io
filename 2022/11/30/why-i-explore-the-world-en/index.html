Why I Explore The World
Harry Huang
  2022-11-30 04:16:33 2022-11-30 04:16  
  • Intro 

I was born in the late 80s, a small city in Eastern China. I vaguely remembered when I was 4 or 5 years old, I often watched Zhengda Zhongyi (CCTV Travel Program) with my parents. In the early 90s when Chinese people had limited access to foreign information, I was excited to follow travelers via TV Program to see the landscapes and cultures around the world. My first trip abroad was in 2004 for summer holiday with my parents, I traveled across 10 countries in Western Europe, it was also my first time to open up the world.

I started to study abroad in Thailand in 2007. After I graduated from high school, I entered Assumption University (Bangkok) with registered for a Major in Computer Science as a Bachelor’s Degree study. At the same time, I started solo travel during the holidays. I traveled across most parts of Thailand, Myanmar, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, the Philippines, and over 20 provinces in China during my college and gap year after graduating from college.

I kept solo traveling even though I was back in China for work, I traveled to Nepal and India in 2018 and took another 1.5-year-long trip across Laos, Thailand, Nepal, UAE, Lebanon, and Turkey during the Covid-19 Pandemic. This trip was the most unforgettable in my life. I spent over half a year lockdown life in Pokhara, Nepal. Besides that, I kept roaming multiple countries under Covid-19 Outbreaks.

In May 2021, I was safely back home in China after 3-week Covid Quarantine. However, the failure of my jobs and the bad Covid Pandemic situations made me fall into confusion again. I tried to think about my way out, on the one hand, I started to focus on learning and strengthening my skills in Web App Dev, and on the other hand, I was planning my next journey again, also I started to learn about Web3.0 Technology. In my opinion, these are my ways to continue exploring the world. Anyway, difficulties are temporary, and the life is always unlimit ~
