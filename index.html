Timeline (Since 2007)

2007 - 2014

I started to study abroad in Thailand since 2007, and I was studied at Assumption University (ABAC) in Bangkok with the major of Computer Science (Bachelor’s Degree). After I finished my studies at ABAC, I spent another two and half years traveling solo and exploring the most part of Thailand.

2015 - 2019

I left Thailand, where I had lived for 7 years, and returned to my homeland. I came to Shenzhen to look for job or startup opportunities, and briefly working as a programmer at an Internet Startup Company. Beyond that, I was traveling solo to explore Myanmar, India, Nepal, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, the Philippines, and also Thailand, where I once lived.

2020 - 2021

When I just started a new journey again, I encountered the global outbreak of COVID-19, forcing me to pause my travels. I then experienced months-long lockdowns in Nepal and Turkey. Due to the extreme difficulty of return flights to my homeland, I spent nearly a year and a half abroad before finally managing to return home with great difficulty.

2022 - 2025

Upon returning home, the shadow of the global pandemic had not yet dissipated. Because I couldn’t find a suitable new job, that period was the darkest time in my life, continuing until the pandemic restrictions were completely lifted in 2023. Later, I had the privilege to collaborate with Ricky, a foreign teacher from Yale University who had been based in Shenzhen for many years, jointly planning seminars, operating group communities, and creating bilingual educational videos in both Chinese and English. Meanwhile, AI Tech was rapidly iterating and developing. With the emergence of AI tools like ChatGPT, Cursor, and Claude, I began using these tools for efficient creation, combining my strengths in mathematics and programming to officially embark on the path of Indie Development targeting the global markets. Currently, the development of Indie Products is still in its early stages. As my Personal Site, I’ll continuously update the progress of my Indie Products here.
