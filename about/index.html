个人简介 (Introduction)

My name is Harry Huang, a freelancer / non-professional programmer / global roamer / lifelong learner, I was studied abroad and lived in Thailand for 7 years, also been traveling to more than 20 countries. Now, I’m staying in Shenzhen, China.

留学和旅行经历 (Education & Travel Experiences)

  • 2007年，我来到泰国曼谷，在Assumption University主修计算机科学专业(本科)，开始了海外游学生活，同时出于对未知世界的向往，在大学期间利用假期的空闲，我开始独自旅行。大学本科的假期，加上离开大学后的间隔年，我走遍了泰国、缅甸、老挝、柬埔寨、越南、菲律宾和国内超过20个省。后来回国工作后，我又利用闲余时间去了尼泊尔和印度，2019年底新冠疫情爆发，当时在国外探索游学线路，因为疫情突发随之而来的全球性封锁困在国外近一年半，酝酿已久的旅行项目也胎死腹中，同时在这一年多，我走访了老挝、泰国、尼泊尔、阿联酋、黎巴嫩、土耳其，记录下疫情当中各国的社会百态，其中最难忘的是在尼泊尔博卡拉长达半年多的lockdown生活。现在，我已平安回到国内，目前居住在深圳，虽然新冠疫情依然在蔓延，但是它无法阻挡我对未来世界的美好向往，为和平祈祷 ～

  • I started to study abroad to Thailand since 2007 after I graduated from high school, I entered to Assumption University (Bangkok) with registered the Major of Computer Science as Bachelor Degree study. At the same time, I started solo travel during holidays. I traveled across the majority part of Thailand, Myanmar, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, Philippines and over 20 provinces in China during the college and gap year after graduated from college. I kept solo traveling even I back in China for works, I traveled to Nepal and India in 2018, and comes another 1.5-year long trip across Laos, Thailand, Nepal, UAE, Lebanon, Turkey during Covid-19 Pandemic, this trip is the most unforgettable in my life, especially I spent over half year lockdown life in Pokhara, Nepal, and beside that I kept roaming multiple countries under Covid-19 Outbreaks. Now, I’m staying in Shenzhen (China) safely, even the Covid-19 continue spreads with strict lockdown, in fact they can’t stop me roaming the future world any more, keep praying for peace and love ~
