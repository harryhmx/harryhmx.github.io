My name is Harry Huang, I’m an IT indie developer / global roamer / lifelong learner, I was studied abroad and lived in Thailand for 7 years, and I’ve been traveling to more than 20 countries. Now, I’m staying in Shenzhen, China.

留学和旅行经历 (Education & Travel Experiences)

  • 2007年，我来到泰国曼谷，在Assumption University主修计算机科学专业(本科)，开始了海外游学生活，同时出于对未知世界的向往，在大学期间利用假期的空闲，我开始独自旅行。大学本科的假期，加上离开大学后的间隔年，我走遍了泰国、缅甸、老挝、柬埔寨、越南、菲律宾和国内超过20个省。后来回国工作后，我又利用闲余时间去了尼泊尔和印度，2019年底新冠疫情爆发，当时在国外探索游学线路，因为疫情突发随之而来的全球性封锁困在国外近一年半，酝酿已久的旅行项目也胎死腹中，同时在这一年多，我走访了老挝、泰国、尼泊尔、阿联酋、黎巴嫩、土耳其，记录下疫情当中各国的社会百态，其中最难忘的是在尼泊尔博卡拉长达半年多的lockdown生活。现在，我已平安回到国内，目前居住在深圳，虽然新冠疫情依然在蔓延，但是它无法阻挡我对未来世界的美好向往，为和平祈祷 ～

  • I started to study abroad in Thailand in 2007. After I graduated from high school, I entered Assumption University (Bangkok) with registered for a Major in Computer Science as a Bachelor’s Degree study. At the same time, I started solo travel during the holidays. I traveled across most parts of Thailand, Myanmar, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, the Philippines, and over 20 provinces in China during my college and gap year after graduating from college. I kept solo traveling even though I was back in China for work, I traveled to Nepal and India in 2018 and took another 1.5-year-long trip across Laos, Thailand, Nepal, UAE, Lebanon, and Turkey during the Covid-19 Pandemic. This trip was the most unforgettable in my life. I spent over half a year lockdown life in Pokhara, Nepal. Besides that, I kept roaming multiple countries under Covid-19 Outbreaks. Now, I’m safely staying in Shenzhen (China), and even the Covid-19 spreads with the strict lockdown. They can’t stop me from roaming the future world anymore. Keep praying for peace and love ~
